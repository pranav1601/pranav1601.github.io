Over 68000 people die from disasters every year. And globally we incur hundreds of billions of dollars in losses.

Generate an alert from anywhere, anytime. Inform the officials just by a tap.

Place a call to any official in case of emergency easily via AGORA.IO

Previous Next


Project-Insight aims at delivering an efficient solution to post-disaster management problems. After a disaster strikes a city, it takes huge amount of time for the rebuilding of the entire place. One reason for that is the officials take a lot of time to figure out where the disaster has impacted the most and also which part of the city needs it the most. With the help of this app, the people can send an alert just by a tap. This would notify the official about the location of the person and the type of rebuilding desired. This app also provides video calling and broadcasting, which would be required by the officials in times of providing information related to precationary measures and emergency situations.
